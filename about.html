About Me

My journey from Web Development to Data Analytics.

My Story

My journey into tech started with my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). I’ve always liked solving puzzles, which naturally led me into web development as a TYPO3 Developer. While I enjoyed building things for the web, I realized what truly fascinated me was what happened behind the scenes—the data driving the applications.

That realization sparked my transition into the world of data.

My Approach

I approach data analytics as the key to unlocking business value. Because of my developer background, I hate manual, repetitive work. My goal in any team is to automate routine reporting processes, ensure 100% data accuracy, and visually communicate insights that drive growth.

To continuously upskill and stay ahead of the curve, I have earned certifications in Advanced SQL from HackerRank and Python from Kaggle. This allows me to bring modern analytics techniques—like advanced data modeling, data cleaning, and interactive visualization—straight into business reporting.

Beyond the Screen

When I’m not writing queries or optimizing Excel sheets, I’m usually tracking tech trends, exploring open-source tools, or working on custom scripts to see what else I can automate.
